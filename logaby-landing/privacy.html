Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Logaby ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Data Storage

Your baby's data stays on your device. Logaby stores all tracking data (feedings, diapers, sleep, weight) locally on your iPhone using Apple's secure on-device storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to use the Family Sync feature.

Family Sync Feature

If you choose to sync data with family members:

  • Data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers
  • Only family members with your invite code can access your data
  • You can leave a family group at any time
  • Leaving a family removes your access to synced data but preserves your local data

Voice Data

When you use voice logging:

  • Your voice is converted to text on your device
  • The text (not audio) is sent to our AI service to parse the activity
  • We do not store voice recordings
  • Parsed activities are stored locally on your device (or synced if using Family Sync)

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • Email address — Only if you join our waitlist or contact support
  • Usage analytics — Anonymous, aggregated data to improve the app (no personal information)
  • Purchase information — Handled entirely by Apple; we only receive confirmation of purchase status

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your baby's name or personal details
  • Your location
  • Photos or videos
  • Contacts
  • Voice recordings

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — For Family Sync data storage (only if you opt in)
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — For voice text parsing (text only, not stored)
  • Apple — For in-app purchases and app distribution

Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored until you delete the app
  • Family Sync data: Stored while you're part of a family; deleted when you leave
  • Waitlist emails: Retained until you unsubscribe

Children's Privacy

Logaby is designed for parents and caregivers, not children. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all local data by deleting the app
  • Leave a family group to stop cloud syncing
  • Request deletion of any cloud data by contacting us
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
